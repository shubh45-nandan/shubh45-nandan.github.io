About Me

Hello! I'm a third-year undergraduate student in Artificial Intelligence and Robotics at Dayananda Sagar University, India. My primary interests lie at the intersection of machine learning, robotics, and intelligent systems, with a strong focus on applying learning-based methods to real-world, high-impact problems.

My current research work centers on medical image analysis, particularly histopathology-based cancer classification using deep convolutional neural networks. I have worked extensively with architectures such as EfficientNet, ResNet, DenseNet, and MobileNet, and have explored hybrid learning approaches that combine deep learning with reinforcement learning agents (including PPO, SAC, DQN, and A2C) to improve classification robustness and performance. Several of these projects are being prepared for research paper submissions.

Alongside this, I actively work on robotics-oriented problems, including path planning and decision-making, where I am interested in how classical algorithms and learning-based methods can be integrated for intelligent navigation and control. I am also exploring cognitive architectures that model perception, reasoning, and action as a unified system, with the goal of building more adaptive and interpretable intelligent agents.

In parallel with my research, I consistently practice Data Structures and Algorithms, strengthening my foundations in problem-solving, complexity analysis, and efficient system design. This work directly supports my research by improving my ability to reason about scalability, optimization, and performance-aware implementations of AI systems.

More broadly, my interests include:

  • Deep learning and representation learning
  • Computer vision for healthcare and robotics
  • Reinforcement learning and sequential decision-making
  • Path planning, cognitive architectures, and intelligent agents
  • Data structures, algorithms, and scalable AI systems

I am particularly motivated by research that balances theoretical understanding with rigorous implementation, and I enjoy deeply analyzing model behavior, algorithmic trade-offs, and system-level design rather than treating models as black boxes.

I actively seek opportunities to collaborate on research projects, open-source contributions, and academic discussions, especially with researchers and practitioners working in machine learning, robotics, and applied AI.

Please feel free to reach out for research collaborations or discussions. The best way to reach me is via email: shubh442005@gmail.com
